SG Land Transport currently shows information about bus stops and bus arrival times.
- Nearby bus stops: Show all bus stops in near proximity (requires permission to track your location)
- Favorite bus stops: Personalize your experience and store frequently used bus stops as favorites to easily access them at any time
- Search: Search for bus stops by name, bus stop code or address
- Bus Arrival times: For each bus stop it will show you which buses it serves, the next 3 estimated arrivals for each bus, the type of the bus and the available space

More features to come soon!

Best of all, SG Land Transport is and always will be:
- 100% free
- 100% ad-free
- 100% open source
