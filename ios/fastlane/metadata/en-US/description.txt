SG Land Transport shows information about bus stops and bus arrival times.
- Nearby bus stops: Find bus stops that are in near proximity (requires permission to track your location)
- Favorite bus services: Personalize your experience and store frequently used bus services as favorites to easily access them at any time
- Bus Arrival times: For each bus service it will show you the next 3 estimated arrivals for each bus and the available space
- Search: Search for bus stops by name, bus stop code or address
- Bus Routes: See bus routes along with bus service details like bus operator arrival frequency and more 

More features to come soon!

Best of all, SG Land Transport is and always will be:
- 100% free
- 100% ad-free
- 100% open source
